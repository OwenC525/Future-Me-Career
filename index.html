Cyber Security Engineer
Career Overview

Working as a Cybersecurity Engineer, you can expect to create software that protects information and systems from cyber threats like malware, ransomware, and hacking. They also work in designing, implementing, and managing security measures to protect an organization's data, systems, and networks. They typically work in an office environment, but some may work remotely depending on their organization. They work to protect sensitive information and prevent data from being stolen or taken advantage of.
